Institutions

This page will give you more information about Portland's two- and four-year institutions. But first, you might be wondering what the difference between two- and four-year institutions is in the first place. Two-year institutions, more commonly known as community colleges, are schools where students receive a degree called an Associates Degree, usually in about two years. On the other hand, students at four-year institutions typically earn Bachelors degrees over a four year time period. Not sure which is the best fit for you? Click here to read more about how to choose between two- and four-year colleges and universities. Once you've decided, see below to get connected to some local options right here in Portland!
