Welcome to College Guide Portland!

The goal of this website is to direct high school students, parents/guardians, and guidance counselors in the Portland area towards information about higher education. If you're interested in learning more about the schools Portland has to offer, the scholarships you're eligible for, community members and organizations who can help you with your application or connect you to resources, need to look at a list of what is needed for a successful and complete application, or want to chat with a current college student about his or her experience, College Guide Portland is the place for you. Each section of the website is briefly described below, so feel free to explore and get excited about college!
